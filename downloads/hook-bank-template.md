## How to Use This Template

**What this is:** 100+ hooks organized by awareness stage. Each hook has been tested and proven across DTC categories.

**How to use it:**
1. Identify your audience's awareness stage
2. Choose 3-5 hooks from that section
3. Adapt to your product (fill in the blanks)
4. Test and iterate

## Awareness Stage Guide

| Stage | They Know | Hook Approach |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| **Problem-Aware** | They have a problem | Agitate the problem. Make them feel it. |
| **Solution-Aware** | Solutions exist | Differentiate. Why yours, not theirs? |
| **Product-Aware** | Your product exists | Overcome objections. Create urgency. |
| **Most-Aware** | Everything | Deal, bonus, urgency. Remove friction. |

## Hook Testing Framework

### Step 1: Generate Variants
Choose 3-5 hooks from different patterns. Fill in the blanks.

### Step 2: Internal Review
Read each aloud. Does it:
- [ ] Stop you in 3 seconds?
- [ ] Create curiosity or emotion?
- [ ] Lead naturally to the rest of the script?

### Step 3: External Test
Show hooks to 3 people who match your target:
- "What did you hear?"
- "What emotion did you feel?"
- "Would you keep watching?"

### Step 4: Iterate
Keep the winner. Kill the rest. Generate 3 new variants based on the winner.
