# Competitor Analysis Framework
## How to Spy on Competitors (Ethically and Effectively)

**The Creative Record** | Version 1.0 | March 2026

---

## Why Competitor Analysis Matters

**The wrong way:** Copy their exact hooks, mimic their style, chase every trend.

**The right way:** Understand what they're testing, learn from positioning, find gaps.

**The goal:** Intelligence, not imitation.

---

## What to Analyze

### 1. Creative Volume & Frequency

**What to track:**
- How many new ads per week/month?
- What formats are they prioritizing?
- Seasonal patterns?

**Why it matters:**
- High volume = aggressive testing budget
- Format priority = what's working

**Track in spreadsheet:**
| Date | Brand | Format | Hook | Notes |
|------|-------|--------|------|-------|

**Tools:**
- Meta Ad Library: facebook.com/ads/library
- TikTok Creative Center: ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter

---

### 2. Creative Lifespan

**What to track:**
- How long do they run the same ad?
- When do they refresh creative?

**Benchmarks:**
| Lifespan | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| 7-14 days | Testing phase |
| 15-30 days | Promising |
| 30+ days | Strong performer |
| 60+ days | Clear winner |

**Track:** Screenshot ads weekly, note first/last seen dates.

---

### 3. Hook Patterns & Messaging

**What to track:**
- What hooks do they use most?
- What awareness stage are they targeting?
- What pain points do they emphasize?

**Categorize hooks:**
- Problem-aware (agitating pain)
- Solution-aware (differentiating)
- Product-aware (social proof)
- Most-aware (deals/urgency)

**Track:**
| Hook Type | Example | Frequency | Your Opportunity |
|-----------|---------|-----------|------------------|
|           |         |           |                  |

---

### 4. Visual Style & Production

**What to track:**
- UGC vs. studio production
- Creator demographics
- Setting/locations
- Color palettes

**Why it matters:**
- Production level = budget & strategy
- Creator type = audience targeting
- Settings = lifestyle positioning

---

### 5. Offers & Positioning

**What to track:**
- Price points they emphasize
- Bundles vs. single products
- Guarantees and risk reversal
- Positioning angles

**Positioning categories:**
- [ ] Price/Value  - [ ] Quality/Premium
- [ ] Convenience  - [ ] Results/Proof
- [ ] Innovation   - [ ] Status/Lifestyle

---

### 6. Landing Pages

**What to track:**
- Where do their ads send traffic?
- Page structure and headlines
- Offer presentation
- Social proof placement

**Check:** Click their ads (in incognito), screenshot landing pages.

## What to Do With This Intelligence

### Find the Gaps
- What pain points are they NOT addressing?
- What creator types are they NOT using?
- What formats are they ignoring?

### Learn From Winners
- Why is their long-running ad working?
- What hook patterns convert for them?
- What can you adapt (not copy)?

### Differentiate
- If they're all doing X, can you do Y?
- Can you be more specific?
- Can you own a different angle?

---

## Analysis Schedule

**Weekly (15 min):**
- Check Meta Ad Library for new ads
- Screenshot anything new
- Note hook patterns

**Monthly (1 hour):**
- Update competitor tracker
- Calculate creative lifespans
- Identify winning patterns
- Plan your response

**Quarterly (2 hours):**
- Deep dive on top 3 competitors
- Landing page analysis
- Full positioning review
- Strategy adjustment

---

## Ethics Reminder

✅ DO:
- Analyze patterns and strategies
- Learn from their testing
- Find gaps and opportunities
- Build your own approach

❌ DON'T:
- Copy their exact creative
- Use their proprietary assets
- Claim their testimonials
- Mimic their brand voice

**Intelligence, not imitation.**
