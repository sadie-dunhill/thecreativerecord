Common Questions

FAQ

The questions we get most often — answered simply.

I bought a skill but I can't find the download email. Where is it?

Check your spam or junk folder. The subject line is "Your Creative Record Skills Are Ready." If it's not there after 5 minutes, email sadie@goodostudios.com with your order details and I'll send it directly.

I downloaded a .zip file. How do I open it?

On a Mac or Windows PC: double-click it. It will automatically open (or "unzip") into a regular folder. Nothing installs — it just turns the compressed file into a normal folder you can browse. On a phone: tap the file and it will expand automatically.

I can't open the .md files. What do I do?

On a Mac: right-click the .md file → "Open With" → TextEdit. On Windows: right-click → "Open With" → Notepad. On a phone: tap the file and it should open as a text document. If none of those work, you can also drag the .md file into Google Docs — it'll open and be fully readable.

Do I need any special software to use these?

No. The HTML files (.html) open in any web browser you already have. The markdown files (.md) open in any text editor. No special apps, no subscriptions, no accounts. Everything works with what's already on your computer or phone.

Can I use these on my phone?

Yes. HTML files open in Safari or Chrome on your phone. The .md files open as text files. For the best experience when filling in templates, we recommend a computer — but reading and reference works great on any device.

Where do the files go after I download them?

They save to your Downloads folder (on Mac or Windows: that's usually in your home folder under "Downloads"). On an iPhone/iPad: they go into the Files app under "Downloads." You can move them anywhere you like after that.

Do the skills expire? Will I lose access?

No. The files are yours permanently once you download them. They live on your computer, not in the cloud. They don't require internet to use and they never expire.

I'm not sure I'm using the framework correctly. How do I know?

The best test: read the completed example in the EXAMPLES/ folder, then compare it to what you created. If yours follows the same structure, you're doing it right. If you're still unsure, email your work to sadie@goodostudios.com and I'll take a look.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, within 30 days of purchase. Email sadie@goodostudios.com with your order details. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through. Full details at thecreativerecord.com/refund.

I want to use this for client work, not just my own brand. Is that allowed?

Yes. Your purchase includes a personal use license that covers your own business and your direct client work. You may not resell or redistribute the frameworks themselves.
