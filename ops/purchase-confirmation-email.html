Here's exactly what to do after you download:

1

Click the button above and save the folder

It will download to your Downloads folder. On a phone, it saves in the Files app.

2

Open the folder

If it downloads as a .zip file, double-click it to open it. Nothing installs — it just unpacks into a regular folder.

3

Open README-FIRST.html

Find the file called "README-FIRST.html" and double-click it. It opens in your web browser and tells you exactly what to do next.

💡 The one thing to remember

Always start with README-FIRST.html. It's the guide for everything else. The TEMPLATES/ folder is where you do your work. The EXAMPLES/ folder shows you what "done" looks like before you start.
