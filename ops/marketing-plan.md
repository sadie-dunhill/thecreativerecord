## SECTION 1: Launch Strategy (Week 1-2)

### Soft Launch Framework

**Goal:** Validate product-market fit, gather social proof, and fix friction points before public launch.

**Beta Cohort (10 customers @ 50% off):**

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Target Profile** | DTC founders, creative strategists, freelancers managing ads for brands |
| **How to Recruit** | Personal outreach via Twitter DMs, LinkedIn messages, newsletter blast |
| **Recruitment Timeline** | Days 1-3: Outreach list → Days 4-7: Personal invites → Days 8-14: Onboard |
| **Beta Incentive** | 50% off + 1-on-1 script feedback session (30 min) |
| **What They Get** | Full access to all skills, private Slack channel, direct feedback channel |

**Beta Recruitment Script (Twitter DM):**
```
Hey [Name] — been following your work on [specific thing]. 

I'm launching The Creative Record next week: the exact creative frameworks 
I've used auditing 1000+ ads. 

Doing a soft launch with 10 people at 50% off + personal feedback session. 
Wanted to offer you a spot before I open it publicly.

Interested?
```

**Testimonial Collection Process:**
- Day 3 after purchase: Check-in email
- Day 7: Request testimonial (specific prompt: "What specific result did you get?")
- Day 14: Case study interview (optional, 15 min call)

**Beta Success Metrics:**
- [ ] 8+ of 10 complete a skill
- [ ] 5+ testimonials collected
- [ ] 0 critical bugs
- [ ] 2+ case studies ready

**Go/No-Go Decision (Day 14):**
- Go: 7+ satisfied customers, testimonials collected, no blocking issues
- Delay: Fix issues, extend beta, gather more feedback

**Goal:** Maximize early adoption, gather social proof, create urgency.

| Product | Launch Price | Regular Price | Notes |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| Individual Skills | $29 | $39 | Limited time, all skills same price |
| Full Bundle | $79 | $99 | All 7 skills, best value |
| Subscription (First 100) | $19/mo | $29/mo | Grandfathered pricing |

### Offer Stack

**Core Offer:**
- [ ] Full access to purchased skill(s)
- [ ] Downloadable templates and worksheets
- [ ] Private community access (Slack/Discord)

**Launch Bonuses (First 14 Days):**
- [ ] Free script feedback (1 submission per customer)
- [ ] Bonus: "Hook Bank" — 50 proven hook formulas
- [ ] Bonus: "Creative Strategy Cheat Sheet" PDF

**Urgency Mechanisms:**
1. **Price increase:** Launch price ends Day 14
2. **Bonus expiration:** Free feedback ends Day 14
3. **Subscription cap:** Only 100 spots at $19/mo

### Pricing Communication

**On Sales Page:**
- Strikethrough regular price, show launch price
- Countdown timer to price increase
- "Only X spots left at $19/mo" (dynamic if possible)

**In Emails:**
- Lead with value, then price
- Always anchor against regular price
- Clear deadline for offers
