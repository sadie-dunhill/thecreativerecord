## Pricing Philosophy

The Creative Record follows a value-based pricing model:
- Individual skills priced for impulse purchases
- Bundle creates clear savings incentive
- Membership for ongoing value and community
- Feedback pricing scaled to depth of review

**Key principles:**
1. **Clear savings** at every tier (make math obvious)
2. **No decision paralysis** (only 5 core options)
3. **Launch incentive** (25% off creates urgency)
4. **Upgrade path** (individual → bundle → membership)

## Monthly Membership

**Price:** $29/month

**Positioning:**
- For ongoing learners and practitioners
- Community access as key differentiator
- Feedback included (normally $49-99)
- "Pay for one skill, get everything"

**What's included:**
- Access to ALL skills (current + future)
- 1 micro-feedback per month ($49-99 value)
- Private community access
- Monthly live Q&A
- New skills added (members get free)
- Cancel anytime

**Math for members:**
```
Monthly: $29
Included feedback: $49 value
Net cost for skills access: FREE
```

**Annual cost:** $348/year  
**Break-even point:** After 2 feedback requests

**Recommended for:**
- Active practitioners wanting feedback
- Community learners
- Buyers planning to purchase multiple skills over time
