Common questions

When do I get paid?
Payouts are processed on the 15th of each month for the prior month's confirmed commissions. We pay via Stripe to your bank or PayPal account.
How long does the cookie last?
90 days. If someone clicks your link and purchases within 90 days, you earn the commission -- even if they don't buy on the first visit.
Is there a minimum audience size?
No minimum. We care about audience alignment, not raw size. A 500-person email list of brand founders or marketing directors is more valuable to us than 50k general followers.
Do I need to be a member myself?
Not required, but strongly recommended. The best affiliates recommend things they've actually used. We offer affiliates a discounted membership -- details shared post-approval.
What happens if someone gets a refund?
Commissions are reversed if a purchase is refunded within the 14-day refund window. After that window, your commission is locked in.
Can I promote individual skills and the membership?
Yes. Your affiliate link works for the entire store -- skills, bundles, and membership. You earn 30% on everything.
