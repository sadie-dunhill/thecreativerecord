## Phase 2 — Personalized Skill Creation Service
**Target:** 45–60 days from launch
**Status:** Ready to build now (service page in progress)

### The Concept
Customers upload their internal documents, SOPs, training materials, or IP. The service analyzes and extracts the core frameworks, processes, and knowledge -- then delivers a custom skill document they can use repeatedly.

**Price:** $20/skill (positioned as accessible, high-volume)

### Who It's For
- Agency owners with a creative process they've never documented
- Consultants with a methodology living in their heads
- Companies with internal training that needs to be packaged
- Founders with IP they want to productize

### Deliverables Per Order
1. Custom skill markdown document (structured framework)
2. Implementation guide (how to actually use it)
3. Template files if applicable

### Service Mechanics
- Customer uploads via form (PDF, DOCX, Google Docs link, pasted text)
- 24–48 hour turnaround
- One round of revisions included
- Delivered via email as markdown + PDF

### Phase 2 Revenue Projection
- $20/order × 100 orders/month = $2,000/month
- Low overhead, high margin
- Builds case studies for Phase 3 (course content)
- Volume target to hit before course launch: 50+ completed orders

## Phase 3 — DIY Skill Building Course
**Target:** 90 days from launch
**Status:** Coming soon (waitlist page in progress)

### The Concept
Teach people how to build their own skills -- not just use them. This serves the audience who wants to package their own expertise and either use it internally or sell it.

**Price:** $49 pre-launch / $99 regular

### Curriculum Outline

**Module 1: What Makes a Great Skill**
- Frameworks vs. templates vs. checklists
- The anatomy of a skill that actually gets used
- Common mistakes that make skills useless

**Module 2: Extract the Knowledge**
- How to pull frameworks out of your brain
- The interview method for documenting processes
- From case studies to teachable systems

**Module 3: Build the Skill**
- Structure for clarity and action
- Writing for someone who doesn't know your context
- Templates, examples, and the implementation guide

**Module 4: Package and Distribute**
- Formats that work (markdown, Notion, PDF, web)
- How to price a skill
- How to sell it, share it, or use it internally
- Building a skill marketplace presence

**Format:** 4 modules × 3–4 video lessons each + workbook + templates

### Phase 3 Revenue Projection
- Pre-launch: $49 × 200 waitlist conversions = $9,800
- Regular: $99 × 50 sales/month = $4,950/month
- Potential: Ongoing passive income with periodic cohort launches
