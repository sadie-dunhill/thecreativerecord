# SMB Content Calendar — The Creative Record

**Document Owner:** Sadie Dunhill  
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026  
**Cadence:** 2x/week (Monday + Thursday)  
**Audience:** True SMBs -- local businesses, service providers, solopreneurs, small ecommerce  
**Publishing Format:** Blog post + email newsletter excerpt + 1 social clip  

---

## Overview

This calendar covers 12 weeks of SMB-focused content. Every post is written for the business owner doing it themselves -- not a marketing team, not an agency. Each piece has a primary keyword target, a lead magnet tie-in, and a clear "what they can do after reading this."

**Themes by month:**
- Month 1 (Weeks 1-4): Getting Started + Foundation
- Month 2 (Weeks 5-8): Templates + Local + Service Business
- Month 3 (Weeks 9-12): Advanced Tactics + Scale

## Content Distribution Protocol

### For Every Post Published:

**Day 0 (Publish):**
- [ ] Post goes live with correct schema
- [ ] Internal links to related posts are active
- [ ] Lead magnet CTA is placed correctly (inline + end of post)
- [ ] Push to newsletter (excerpt format, not full post)

**Day 1:**
- [ ] Share in 1 SMB-relevant Facebook Group (manual, with value)
- [ ] Post excerpt on LinkedIn or Instagram with link

**Week 1:**
- [ ] Submit to 1 SMB resource directory or community

**Month 1:**
- [ ] Check Search Console for early impressions on target keyword
- [ ] Identify if post needs FAQs added for featured snippet
