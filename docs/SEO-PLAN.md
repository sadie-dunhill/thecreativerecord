**Document Owner:** Sadie Dunhill

**Niche:** Creative strategy, ad copywriting, DTC advertising  
**Goal:** Drive organic traffic that converts to skill sales and Script Desk subscriptions

## Executive Summary

The Creative Record has a structural SEO advantage most marketing resources don't: it's built around specific, searchable frameworks (Schwartz stages, hook formulas, UGC briefs) that practitioners actively Google for. The strategy is to own the informational end of those searches, pull people into the skill funnel, and convert with the most useful free download they've ever found.

Competitors (Demand Curve, Marketing Examples, Swipe File, Copyhackers) either go too broad or lack depth on ad-specific creative frameworks. That's the gap.

**12-Week North Star:**
- 500+ organic sessions/month by Week 12
- 5 cornerstone articles ranking in Google's top 10 for primary targets
- Email list growing 50+ new subs/week from organic alone

### 2.2 Meta Description Template

**Formula:** [Primary keyword as verb phrase] + [specific value/differentiator] + [CTA with soft urgency]

**Examples:**

*Homepage:*
"The creative frameworks behind 1,000+ ad audits for DTC brands. Download script templates, hook banks, and creative briefs. Start converting more traffic today."
*(158 chars)*

*Video Script Framework:*
"A proven video ad script template used across 200+ campaigns. Beat-by-beat structure for Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube ads. Download free preview."
*(149 chars)*

*Hook Bank:*
"50+ hook formulas organized by awareness stage and format. Stop rewriting the same 3 hooks -- download the complete bank for DTC ad creative."
*(142 chars)*

### 2.3 Header Structure

**Every page must have:**
- One H1 -- primary keyword + brand voice, not stuffed
- H2s for major sections -- use secondary keywords naturally
- H3s for subsections -- no need to force keywords here
- H4s only if the page is genuinely long (2,500+ words)

**H1 examples by page:**

| Page | H1 |
|------|----|
| Homepage | "The Creative Frameworks Behind Ads That Actually Sell" |
| Video Script | "A Video Ad Script Template Built for Conversion" |
| Hook Bank | "Hook Formulas That Stop the Scroll" |
| UGC Brief | "The UGC Brief Template Creators Actually Follow" |
| Blog post on hooks | "8 Hook Formulas Every DTC Brand Should Test (With Examples)" |

### 2.4 Internal Linking Map

Build a hub-and-spoke structure. Every blog post links back to a skill. Every skill links to related posts.

```
Homepage
├── /skills/ (hub)
│   ├── /skills/video-script-framework/ → links to blog posts on scripting
│   ├── /skills/hook-bank-template/ → links to blog posts on hooks
│   ├── /skills/ugc-brief-template/ → links to blog posts on UGC
│   ├── /skills/creative-audit-checklist/ → links to creative fatigue content
│   └── /skills/competitor-analysis-framework/ → links to competitive content
└── /blog/ (hub)
    ├── Post: Hook formulas → links to Hook Bank skill
    ├── Post: Script writing → links to Video Script skill
    ├── Post: UGC briefing → links to UGC Brief skill
    ├── Post: Creative fatigue → links to Audit Checklist skill
    └── Post: Schwartz stages → links to multiple skills
```

**Anchor text rules:**
- Use descriptive anchors ("video ad script template," not "click here")
- Max 1-2 links per skill per article
- Link to skill sales page, not just the download

## Part 4: Content Strategy

### 4.1 Cornerstone Content (Build First)

These four articles are the foundation. They should be long (2,000-3,000 words), exhaustive on the topic, and link to skills.

**Cornerstone 1: Hook Formulas**
- Target keyword: "hook formulas for ads"
- Secondary: "how to write a hook for an ad," "DTC ad hooks"
- Angle: Exhaustive taxonomy of hook types with real examples from each
- CTA: Link to Hook Bank skill (natural upsell)
- Content upgrade: Free PDF of 10 hook templates

**Cornerstone 2: Video Ad Script Writing**
- Target keyword: "how to write video ad scripts that convert"
- Secondary: "video ad script template," "video script for Facebook ads"
- Angle: Beat-by-beat guide, not just advice -- show the actual structure
- CTA: Link to Video Script Framework skill
- Content upgrade: Downloadable script template

**Cornerstone 3: UGC Brief Writing**
- Target keyword: "how to write a UGC brief," "UGC brief template"
- Secondary: "how to brief a UGC creator," "creator brief for ads"
- Angle: The brief that creators actually follow -- what to include, what to cut
- CTA: Link to UGC Brief Template skill
- Content upgrade: Brief checklist

**Cornerstone 4: Schwartz Awareness Stages**
- Target keyword: "Schwartz awareness stages," "Eugene Schwartz advertising"
- Secondary: "5 stages of awareness marketing," "awareness stage advertising"
- Angle: Applied to modern DTC ads with real brand examples
- CTA: Multiple skills linked (different hooks work at different stages)
- Content upgrade: Awareness stage worksheet
