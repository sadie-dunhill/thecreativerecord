Refund Policy

We stand behind our work. If it's not right for you, we make it right.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with a purchase for any reason, email us within 30 days and we'll refund you in full. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.

Last updated: March 15, 2026

How to Request a Refund

The process is simple. Here's exactly what to do:

  • 1

    Email us within 30 days of purchase

    Send a message to sadie@goodostudios.com from the email address you used to purchase. Subject line: "Refund Request."

  • 2

    Include your order details

    Include your order number or the email address used at checkout. This helps us locate your purchase quickly.

  • 3

    We'll confirm and process

    We'll confirm receipt within one business day and initiate the refund through Stripe. You don't need to explain why -- though feedback is always welcome.

  • 4

    Refund appears in 5-10 business days

    Once processed, refunds typically appear on your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your card issuer.

Eligible Purchases

Our 30-day guarantee applies to:

  • Individual skill purchases
  • Complete skill bundles
  • Script Desk sessions (prorated refund for unused scripts in your desk)

Processing Timeline

Once we initiate a refund:

  • You'll receive an email confirmation from us within 1 business day
  • Stripe processes the refund within 2 business days
  • Your card issuer typically applies the credit within 5-10 business days
  • Total time from request to card credit: typically 7-12 business days

Script Desk Refunds

For monthly membership subscribers:

  • Cancel anytime through your account settings at /account
  • Cancellation stops future billing immediately
  • Refund requests within 30 days of any charge will be honored in full
  • Access to membership content continues until the end of your paid billing period

Exceptions

We keep exceptions to a minimum. We will decline a refund request only if:

  • The request is received more than 30 days after the original purchase date
  • The account has been flagged for abuse (e.g., repeatedly purchasing and refunding, sharing purchased content)

If you're outside the 30-day window but have a genuine issue with a product, email us anyway. We'll do our best to make it right.

Contact

Refund questions or requests:

  • Email: sadie@goodostudios.com
  • Response time: Within 1 business day
