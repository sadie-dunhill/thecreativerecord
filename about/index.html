About The Creative Record

Built by someone who has analyzed 1,000+ DTC ad creatives so you don't have to guess.

The Problem

Most DTC brands are guessing with their creative. They hire agencies that charge $20K for 3 concepts. They sit through 6-hour courses that teach theory but don't give them tools. They copy competitors without understanding why the creative works.

The result? Ads that look good but don't convert. Content that fills feeds but doesn't find customers. Budget spent on production that doesn't perform.

The Solution

The Creative Record is a collection of skills -- downloadable frameworks, templates, and checklists that you can use immediately. No theory. No fluff. Just the exact tools used by brands that scale.

"I don't teach creative strategy. I document what works and give you the tools to do it yourself."

What Makes This Different

1. It's Based on Real Analysis

Every framework in The Creative Record comes from analyzing actual winning creatives. Not theory. Not "best practices." Real ads that real brands are running right now. I've reverse-engineered what works and packaged it into usable tools.

2. It's Immediately Usable

You don't need to watch hours of video or read a 200-page book. Download the skill, open it, use it. Most people apply what they learn within 24 hours of downloading.

3. It's Affordable

$39 for a skill that took weeks to develop. $29/month for ongoing access to everything. Compare that to a $5K agency retainer or a $2K course. The value is ridiculous because the delivery is efficient.
