# EXAMPLE: Completed Script — Supplement Brand

*A second example so you can see the framework applied to a different product category.*

---

## Product Info (Filled In)

1. **What does your product do?**
   → A daily magnesium supplement that improves sleep quality

2. **Who is it for?**
   → Adults 35–55 who lie awake at night even when they're exhausted

3. **What problem does it solve?**
   → Racing mind at bedtime, can't fall asleep even when bone tired

4. **What's the biggest result?**
   → Fall asleep faster, stay asleep through the night

5. **What makes it different?**
   → Uses magnesium glycinate (better absorbed) not magnesium oxide (cheap version that doesn't work)

6. **Best customer result?**
   → "Fell asleep within 20 minutes for the first time in years" — 1,200+ reviews

7. **What to do after watching?**
   → Try it risk-free with 30-day money back guarantee

8. **URL/offer?**
   → deepsleepco.com — 30-day guarantee

---

## Completed Script

**BEAT 1: THE HOOK (0–3 seconds)**

> "You're exhausted. You lie down. And then your brain turns on."

---

**BEAT 2: THE PROBLEM (3–8 seconds)**

> "Most sleep problems aren't a sleep problem. They're a magnesium problem. And most magnesium supplements use a cheap form your body can't even absorb."

---

**BEAT 3: THE PRODUCT (8–15 seconds)**

> "Deep Sleep Magnesium uses magnesium glycinate — the form doctors actually recommend. One capsule, 30 minutes before bed. That's it."

---

**BEAT 4: THE PROOF (15–25 seconds)**

> "Over 1,200 reviews. People say they fall asleep within 20 minutes when it used to take 2 hours. No groggy feeling in the morning. Just sleep."

---

**BEAT 5: THE CALL TO ACTION (25–30 seconds)**

> "Try it risk-free at deepsleepco.com. If you don't sleep better in 30 days, full refund. No questions."

---

## Timing Check

Approximately 28 seconds out loud. ✓

---

## What Makes This Work

- **Beat 1** opens with pure emotion — anyone who's experienced this knows exactly what that moment feels like
- **Beat 2** reframes the problem (it's a magnesium deficiency, not just "bad sleep") and plants doubt about competitors
- **Beat 3** uses the technical differentiator (glycinate vs. oxide) in plain language — "the form doctors actually recommend"
- **Beat 4** leads with scale (1,200 reviews) then a specific result (20 minutes vs. 2 hours)
- **Beat 5** removes risk entirely with a 30-day guarantee — the last objection, handled
