# 20 Hook Starters — Copy, Paste, Adapt

*Stuck on your hook? Pick any of these and swap in your product details.*

---

## Problem-Calling Hooks (Best for most products)

1. "If you've tried [PRODUCT CATEGORY] and nothing worked, this is why."
2. "The real reason [COMMON PROBLEM] keeps coming back."
3. "Stop doing [COMMON MISTAKE]. Here's what actually works."
4. "You've been using [PRODUCT CATEGORY] wrong — and it's costing you [RESULT]."
5. "Why most [PRODUCT CATEGORY] products fail (and what to use instead)."

---

## Result-First Hooks (Great for transformation products)

6. "[RESULT] in [TIME] — here's exactly how."
7. "I [IMPRESSIVE RESULT] without [THING PEOPLE HATE]. Here's what I did."
8. "From [STARTING POINT] to [END RESULT] in [TIME]. This is what changed."
9. "[NUMBER] people did this and got [RESULT]. It only takes [TIME]."
10. "What if [DESIRED RESULT] was actually possible without [SACRIFICE]?"

---

## Question Hooks (Creates instant curiosity)

11. "Do you know what's actually in your [PRODUCT CATEGORY]?"
12. "Why is nobody talking about [THING]?"
13. "What if everything you know about [TOPIC] is wrong?"
14. "How do [IMPRESSIVE PEOPLE] actually [DO THING]?"
15. "What does [DESIRABLE OUTCOME] actually require?"

---

## Identity Hooks (Great for lifestyle brands)

16. "This one is for [SPECIFIC PERSON] who [RELATABLE STRUGGLE]."
17. "If you're a [IDENTITY] who [DOES THING], you need to see this."
18. "Nobody makes products for [SPECIFIC PERSON]. So we did."
19. "This is what [IMPRESSIVE PEOPLE] use for [RESULT]. Now it's for everyone."
20. "The [CATEGORY] product that finally gets [SPECIFIC PERSON]."

---

## How to Use These

1. Pick a hook that feels right for your product
2. Fill in the brackets with your real details
3. Read it out loud — does it make YOU want to keep watching?
4. If yes, you're done. If no, try another one.

**The test:** Show it to someone who doesn't know your product. Do they ask "wait, what is this?" If yes, it's working.
