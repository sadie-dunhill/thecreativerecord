What This Helps You Do

Write a 30-second video ad script for any product — even if you've never written an ad in your life.

When to Use It

Any time you need to create a video ad or short promotional video and don't know where to start.

3-Step Process

1
Read an example first Open EXAMPLES/example-skincare-brand.md and read the whole thing. It takes 3 minutes. You'll immediately understand the structure.
2
Fill in your product details Open TEMPLATES/script-template-blank.md. Answer the 8 questions at the top about your product. Takes 10 minutes.
3
Write one beat at a time Write Beat 1 (hook), then Beat 2, and so on. Each beat has instructions. Read it aloud when done — aim for 28–32 seconds.

Example — Skincare Brand

Completed Script (29 seconds)
Beat 1 (Hook): "If you're still breaking out in your 30s — this is why."
Beat 2 (Problem): "Adult acne is hormonal. Most skincare products are still treating you like you're 16."
Beat 3 (Product): "Clear Skin Co. uses willow bark and niacinamide — ingredients that work with your cycle, not against it."
Beat 4 (Proof): "847 reviews, 4.8 stars. Most common result: 'First clear month in years.'"
Beat 5 (CTA): "Visit clearskinco.com — code FIRST20 for 20% off."
