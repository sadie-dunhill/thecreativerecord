How to Use This — Step by Step

1

Read one example 5 min

Open the EXAMPLES/ folder. Read "example-skincare-brand.md" (or any example). Don't overthink it — just read it like a story. You'll see how the 5 beats flow together.

2

Open your blank template 2 min

Open the TEMPLATES/ folder. Open "script-template-blank.md". This is your working document. Keep it open.

3

Fill in your product details 10 min

The template has brackets like [YOUR PRODUCT] and [YOUR HOOK]. Replace every bracket with your own words. There are only 8 things to fill in.

4

Write your script using the 5-beat structure 20 min

The framework document shows you exactly what goes in each beat. Beat 1 is your hook (first 3 seconds). Beat 5 is your call to action. Write one beat at a time.

5

Read it out loud and time it 5 min

Read your script aloud and time it. It should be 28–32 seconds. If it's too long, cut from beats 2 and 3 first.

💡 One thing to remember You do NOT need to understand advertising to use this. The framework does the thinking for you. Your only job is to fill in what's true about your product.
