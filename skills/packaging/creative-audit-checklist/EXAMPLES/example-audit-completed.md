# EXAMPLE: Completed Audit — Supplement Brand Ad

*This shows a real audit score with diagnosis. Use it to understand how scoring works.*

---

**Ad audited:** Deep Sleep Magnesium — 30-second video ad  
**Date:** March 2026

---

## Section 1: Hook (First 3 Seconds)

| # | What to Check | Score | Notes |
|---|---------------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Does it stop scrolling? | 4 | Opens with a close-up of someone staring at the ceiling — relatable visual |
| 2 | Is there a clear hook? | 3 | "Having trouble sleeping?" — functional but generic |
| 3 | Does it speak to the right person? | 4 | Clearly targets adults with sleep problems |
| 4 | Is it specific? | 2 | "Trouble sleeping" is too vague — doesn't name the specific feeling |
| 5 | Does it avoid starting with logo? | 5 | Logo doesn't appear until the end |

**Section 1 total: 18 / 25**

**Diagnosis:** The hook is functional but not great. "Having trouble sleeping?" is a question everyone says — it doesn't stop scrolling the way "You're exhausted but you can't sleep. Why?" would.

## Final Score

| Section | Score | Out of |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Hook | 18 | 25 |
| Problem | 21 | 25 |
| Product | 18 | 25 |
| Proof | 21 | 25 |
| CTA | 20 | 25 |
| **TOTAL** | **98** | **125** |

**Score: 98/125 — Good. Small fixes needed.**

---

## Top 3 Things to Fix

1. **Hook specificity (Section 1, point 4):** Change "Having trouble sleeping?" to something like "You're exhausted but your brain won't stop. Here's why." More specific, more emotional.

2. **Differentiator explanation (Section 3, point 13):** Add one sentence: "Most magnesium supplements use magnesium oxide — your body barely absorbs it. This uses magnesium glycinate, the form that actually works."

3. **CTA urgency (Section 5, point 23):** Add a 30-day money-back guarantee to the CTA: "Try it risk-free — 30-day guarantee."
