What This Helps You Do

Score any video ad on 25 points and know exactly what to fix — in 20 minutes.

When to Use It

Before spending money on a new ad, or when an existing ad stops performing and you need to know why.

3-Step Process

1
Watch your ad twice before scoring anything First watch: just watch like a normal viewer. Second watch: take notes on what feels weak. Now open TEMPLATES/audit-scorecard-blank.md.
2
Score each of the 25 points (1–5) Watch a third time while scoring. Go section by section: Hook → Problem → Product → Proof → CTA. When in doubt, give a 3.
3
Add up the score and fix the lowest section first The lowest-scoring section is your biggest problem. Fix that one thing. Re-run the audit after you revise.
