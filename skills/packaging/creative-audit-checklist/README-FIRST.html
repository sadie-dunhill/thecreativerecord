Creative Audit Checklist

Find out exactly what's working and what's broken in your ads — in 20 minutes.

What This Does For You

Most people look at their ad results and see numbers — clicks, costs, conversions. They don't know why the numbers look the way they do.

The Creative Audit Checklist helps you look at any ad and diagnose it. Score it on 25 points. Get a number. Know what to fix.

You don't need to be an advertising expert. The checklist tells you what to look for. Your eyes tell you if it's there.

How to Use This — Step by Step

1

Pick one ad to audit 1 min

Start with your best-performing ad or your worst-performing ad — either works. Watch it twice all the way through before scoring anything.

2

Print the scorecard or open it on screen 1 min

Open TEMPLATES/audit-scorecard-blank.md. You'll score 25 points on a 1–5 scale. Score as you watch.

3

Score each section 15 min

The checklist has 5 sections: Hook, Problem, Solution, Proof, and CTA. Score each point 1–5. When you're unsure, score 3 and move on — you can come back.

4

Add up your score and read the diagnosis 3 min

Total all 25 scores. The scorecard has a score key that tells you what your total means and what to fix first.

Score key:

100–125
Excellent. Scale it.
75–99
Good. Small fixes.
50–74
Average. Major revisions.
25–49
Poor. Rebuild it.
💡 Where most ads lose points Section 1 (Hook) is where 80% of bad scores happen. If the first 3 seconds don't stop someone from scrolling, the rest of the ad doesn't matter. Score this section harshly.

✅ You don't need data to do this audit. You just need to watch the ad and answer honest questions. Your gut score is usually right.
