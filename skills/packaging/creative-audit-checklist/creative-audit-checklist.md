## The 25-Point Creative Audit

This checklist helps you identify exactly what's working and what's broken in your creative. Score each point 1-5, then prioritize your fixes.

**Scoring:**
- 5 = Excellent, no changes needed
- 4 = Good, minor optimization possible
- 3 = Average, needs improvement
- 2 = Poor, significant issues
- 1 = Critical, fix immediately

**Total possible score: 125**

---

## Score Analysis

### Your Total Score: ___/125

**Interpretation:**

**110-125: Excellent**
Your creative is dialed in. Minor optimizations only. Test variations to find incremental improvements.

**90-109: Good**
Solid foundation with room for improvement. Focus on your lowest-scoring sections first.

**70-89: Average**
Mixed performance. Several areas need attention. Prioritize the 3 lowest-scoring sections.

**50-69: Below Average**
Significant issues. Likely underperforming. Major revisions recommended.

**Below 50: Critical**
Fundamental problems. Consider starting fresh with new concept.

## Red Flag Checklist

These issues kill performance. Fix immediately if present:

**Critical Issues (Fix Now):**
- [ ] No clear hook in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Product isn't shown clearly
- [ ] No call-to-action
- [ ] Poor lighting or audio quality
- [ ] Offensive or off-brand content
- [ ] Misleading claims

**Major Issues (Fix Soon):**
- [ ] Too long for platform
- [ ] Confusing message
- [ ] No differentiation from competitors
- [ ] Weak or unclear value proposition
- [ ] Boring or slow pacing
- [ ] Too much text on screen

**Minor Issues (Optimize When Possible):**
- [ ] Could be more engaging
- [ ] Minor audio/visual improvements
- [ ] CTA could be stronger
- [ ] More proof could be added

## Quick Wins (Apply Today)

**If your hook scores low:**
- Add a pattern interrupt in first second
- Lead with strongest visual
- Cut any slow intro
- Start with audio hook

**If your CTA scores low:**
- Make it one specific action
- Add text overlay with CTA
- Mention it twice (middle and end)
- Remove friction words ("consider" "think about")

**If your clarity scores low:**
- Remove one concept
- Simplify language
- Add text overlay for key points
- Show product more clearly

**If your proof scores low:**
- Add customer quote
- Show specific metric
- Include UGC clip
- Mention number of customers

## Example Audit Walkthrough

**Creative:** 30-second skincare video

**Section 1 (Hook): 15/25**
- Pattern interrupt: 3/5 (decent but not surprising)
- Clarity: 4/5 (clear what it is)

**Issue:** Not differentiated, no curiosity
**Fix:** Lead with unique mechanism, create open loop

**Section 2 (Pacing): 18/25**
- Good energy, could be tighter

**Section 3 (Clarity): 20/25**
- Very clear what product does

**Section 4 (Proof): 12/25**
- Weak social proof, no specific results
**Fix:** Add customer quote, show before/after

**Section 5 (CTA): 15/25**
- Present but weak
**Fix:** Make more specific, add text overlay

**Total: 80/125 (Average)**
**Priority:** Fix differentiation and proof first
