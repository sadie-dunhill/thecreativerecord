What This Helps You Do

Research what competitors are running for ads — and find the gaps they're missing so your ads stand out.

When to Use It

Before creating new ads, or when your existing ads feel like every other brand's ads and you need a fresh angle.

3-Step Process

1
Go to facebook.com/ads/library (free, no login) Search 3–5 competitor brand names. Look at their active ads. Write down the hook (first line) of every ad that's been running 30+ days.
2
Fill in the competitor tracker Open TEMPLATES/competitor-tracker-blank.md. One row per ad. Track: hook, claim, proof, and format. 15 minutes total.
3
Find the gap — what is nobody doing? When you see 5 competitors all saying the same thing, that's the crowded lane. What angle is everyone skipping? What customer is everyone ignoring? That's your opportunity.

Example — What the Gap Looks Like

Adult Acne Skincare Category
EVERYONE IS
Using "say goodbye to acne" and "dermatologist recommended" — no differentiation, all the same message.
NOBODY IS
Talking to women in their 30s with hormonal breakouts specifically — treating adult acne as a different problem than teenage acne.
THE AD
"I'm 34 and I break out worse now than in high school. Turns out that's a completely different problem."
