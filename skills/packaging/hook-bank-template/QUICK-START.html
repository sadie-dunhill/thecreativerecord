What This Helps You Do

Find the perfect first line for any video ad — from 100+ proven hooks, organized by what your customer already knows.

When to Use It

Every time you write a new video ad and need a strong opening line that makes people stop scrolling.

3-Step Process

1
Find your awareness stage Ask: "Does my customer know they have a problem?" Yes → Problem-Aware section. No → Unaware section. Tried other products → Solution-Aware section.
2
Pick 3 hooks that feel right Open hook-bank-template.md and read your section. When a hook makes you think "that's my customer" — pick it. Choose 3 to test.
3
Fill in your specific details Replace every [BRACKET] with your actual product, result, or timeframe. Specific beats vague every time. "17 lbs" beats "lose weight."

3 Example Hooks

Adapted Hooks — 3 Categories
SKINCARE — Problem-Aware
"Still breaking out in your 30s? Here's why your cleanser is making it worse."
Why it works: Specific age + blames the wrong solution they're already using
SUPPLEMENT — Solution-Aware
"I was sleeping 8 hours and still exhausted. Turns out it wasn't a sleep problem."
Why it works: They've tried the obvious solution and it failed — creates curiosity
FITNESS — Unaware
"The 12-minute routine I've done every morning for 2 years."
Why it works: Specific time + long streak signals credibility, low barrier to try
