How to Use This — Step by Step

1

Answer one question 2 min

Ask yourself: "Does my customer know they have a problem?" If yes, go to the Problem-Aware section. If no, go to Unaware. If they've already tried other products, go to Solution-Aware. The template has a simple guide.

2

Read the hooks for your section 5 min

Each section has 20+ hooks. Read through them. When one makes you think "oh, that's my customer" — that's the one. Don't overthink it. Trust your gut.

3

Adapt it with your product details 5 min

Most hooks have [BRACKETS] where you fill in your product, your result, your time frame. Swap in your specifics. Keep it under 15 words if possible.

4

Test 3 hooks at a time ongoing

Use the tracking template to record which hooks you're testing. Run 3 versions and see which one gets the most people to keep watching. That one becomes your control for the next test.

💡 The secret to hooks The best hook is specific. "Lose weight" is a bad hook. "Lost 17 lbs before my daughter's wedding" is a great hook. Replace every vague word with a specific detail.
