What This Helps You Do

Write a clear, complete brief for any content creator so they deliver exactly what you need — on the first try.

When to Use It

Every time you hire a creator or influencer to make a video about your product.

3-Step Process

1
Open the blank brief template Go to TEMPLATES/ugc-brief-blank.md. Answer Section 1 (product overview) first. Takes 5 minutes.
2
Write 4 talking points as full sentences Section 2 is the most important. Write each point as a complete sentence the creator could almost read directly. Not topics — full sentences.
3
Send it with one instruction Attach the brief and say: "Please confirm you've read all of this before filming." That single line prevents 90% of revision requests.

Example — Talking Points for Skincare Brand

Clear Skin Co. — Required Talking Points
"I've struggled with adult acne for years and nothing was working — always breaking out right before my period."
"Clear Skin Co. uses gentle ingredients that work with your hormonal cycle — no harsh chemicals that peel your skin."
"Within about 6 weeks, I was having my clearest skin in years. No peeling, no dryness."
"If you're an adult still dealing with breakouts, this is different from anything else I've tried."
