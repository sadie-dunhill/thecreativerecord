# EXAMPLE: Completed UGC Brief — Skincare Brand

*Read this to see what a complete, ready-to-send brief looks like.*

---

## Section 1: Product Overview

**What is it?**
→ Clear Skin Co. is an acne treatment system for adult women. It uses willow bark and niacinamide instead of harsh chemicals like benzoyl peroxide.

**Who is it for?**
→ Women 28–42 who still deal with hormonal breakouts — especially around the chin and jawline — despite having tried other products.

**What problem does it solve?**
→ Most acne products are designed for teenagers and use harsh chemicals that cause peeling and redness. Adult hormonal acne needs a different approach.

**What's the main result?**
→ Clearer skin within 6 weeks without peeling, redness, or irritation.

**Any important facts:**
→ 847 reviews, 4.8 stars average. Made in the USA. No parabens, no fragrance, dermatologist-reviewed formula.

---

## Section 2: What to Say (Talking Points)

**Must say (required):**

1. "I've struggled with adult acne for years and nothing was working — my skin was always broken out right before my period."
2. "I found Clear Skin Co. because it uses gentle ingredients that work with your hormonal cycle, not harsh chemicals that just irritate your skin."
3. "Within about 6 weeks, I was having my clearest skin in years. No peeling, no dryness — just calm, clear skin."
4. "If you're an adult still dealing with breakouts, this is different from anything else I've tried."

**Nice to say:**
1. "The reviews were what convinced me — over 800 women left reviews and it's rated 4.8 stars."
2. "I use it morning and night — takes 30 seconds. That's it."

---

## Section 3: What to Show On Camera

**Required shots:**
- [ ] Show the product packaging (hold the box up clearly)
- [ ] Apply one of the products on camera (serum or moisturizer)
- [ ] Close-up of face showing clear skin (good lighting, no heavy filter)

**Recommended setup:**
- Where to film: Bathroom vanity during morning routine OR bedroom with good window light
- Lighting: Natural light strongly preferred — face should be clearly visible
- What to wear: Casual, clean, no big logos — you're at home getting ready

---

## Section 4: What NOT to Do

**Do NOT say:**
- Do not say the product "cures" or "treats" acne — say it "helped clear" your skin
- Do not compare to Proactiv, Differin, or any other brand by name
- Do not make claims about hormones being "fixed" or "balanced"

**Do NOT do:**
- Do not use a heavy beauty filter or skin-smoothing effect — the raw skin is the point
- Do not film in the dark or with harsh overhead fluorescent light
- Do not read off a script visibly — it needs to feel like you're talking to a friend

---

## Section 5: Video Specs

**Length:** 30 to 60 seconds  
**Format:** Vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels  
**Deliverables:** 1 edited video  
**File format:** .mp4 preferred  

---

## Section 6: The Hook (Opening Line)

**Suggested opening line:**
> "I'm 34 and I still break out more than I did in high school — until I found this."
