The Problem

Most video scripts fail before the first cut

You're not bad at writing. You're writing in the wrong order. Most brands lead with the product, bury the hook, and wonder why nobody watches past 3 seconds. The structure is the problem -- and it's fixable.

  • Opening with brand logos, slow establishing shots, or "Hi, I'm [name]" -- all guaranteed scroll-triggers in the wrong direction
  • Listing features when viewers need to feel the problem first. Emotional relevance before logical proof, every time
  • Weak CTAs that say "check us out" instead of giving viewers one clear, specific next step with a reason to act now

The 5-Beat Structure at a Glance
0 – 3s
Beat 1: The Hook
Stop the scroll. Create pattern interrupt. Visual or audio surprise.
3 – 8s
Beat 2: The Setup
Establish context. Make the problem visceral and relatable.
8 – 15s
Beat 3: The Discovery
Introduce your solution naturally. Build credibility.
15 – 25s
Beat 4: The Proof
Show transformation. Specific numbers. Overcome objections.
25 – 30s
Beat 5: The CTA
One specific action. Remove friction. Create real urgency.
