FAQ

Common questions

What's an awareness stage and why does it matter for hooks?
Awareness stage describes how much your audience already knows about their problem and your solution. A hook that works for cold traffic (problem-aware) will fall flat for a retargeting audience (product-aware) -- and vice versa. The bank is organized so you always pick the right hook for the right moment.
Are these hooks ready to use or do I need to adapt them?
Both. Category-specific hooks (skincare, supplements, etc.) can often be used nearly verbatim. The pattern-based hooks are templates with blanks you fill in. Every hook includes guidance on how to adapt it to your product.
Does this cover static ad hooks or just video?
The bank is written for video hooks, but the patterns work for static headlines too. A "Problem Pivot" formula works whether it's an opening line or a headline -- the underlying psychology is the same.
