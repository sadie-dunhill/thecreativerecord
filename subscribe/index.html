Questions

How the Script Desk works

What happens after I book the desk?
You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to the request form and instructions for submitting your first script. No kickoff call. No onboarding session. Fill out the form and your first desk session is in the queue.
How long do I have to use my desk sessions?
Starter Desk (10 scripts) is valid for 3 months. Growth Desk (20 scripts) is valid for 6 months. Scale Desk (50 scripts) is valid for 12 months. Validity starts from the purchase date.
Can I submit multiple requests at once?
Yes. You can batch requests -- useful for launches when you need 5--8 scripts in a short window. Each script still has its own 48--72 hour turnaround from when the brief is submitted. Scale Desk customers get priority queue status.
How do revisions work?
Starter Desk includes 1 revision round per script. Growth Desk includes 2 rounds per script. Scale Desk includes unlimited revisions. Submit revision feedback via form. Each revision round has its own 48-hour turnaround.
What if I don't use all my scripts?
Unused desk sessions expire at the end of your access window (3, 6, or 12 months). If you're running low on time, email us -- in most cases we can extend the window for a reasonable fee rather than let sessions go to waste.
Who writes the scripts?
Sadie Dunhill -- an experienced creative strategist from Goodo Studios. Not a content mill, not rotating freelancers. Every script comes from the same strategist who has seen what converts across hundreds of DTC campaigns.
What do I need to submit a request?
A clear brief: your brand, product, target audience, the awareness stage you're targeting, and any creative context. The request form has structured prompts. The better your brief, the better the script.
What if I need more scripts than my desk allows?
You can book additional desk sessions at any time. They stack -- your remaining sessions from your current desk plus the new ones. Validity resets to the new purchase date for the new batch.
