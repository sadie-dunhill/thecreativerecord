Membership FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?
Monthly memberships cancel anytime, no penalty. Annual memberships retain access until the renewal date when cancelled.
What happens to unused feedback credits?
Credits roll over for up to 3 months. Skip a month and you can submit 2 pieces of creative the following month.
How do I access the community?
After joining, you'll get an invite to our private Circle community -- where members share work, ask questions, and get feedback from peers.
What's the difference between the two plans?
Price only. Annual is $290/year (saving $58, or 2 months free). Monthly is $29/month. Both plans include the same access and benefits.
Do you offer team plans?
Yes. Teams of 3+ get 20% off. Email team@thecreativerecord.com for team pricing and invoicing options.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes. If you're not satisfied within 14 days, we'll issue a full refund. No questions asked.
