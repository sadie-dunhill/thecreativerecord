Membership FAQ

Common questions about joining.

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly memberships can be cancelled anytime with no penalty. Annual memberships can be cancelled anytime and you'll retain access until your renewal date.
What happens to my feedback credits if I don't use them?
Feedback credits roll over for up to 3 months. So if you skip a month, you can submit 2 pieces of creative the next month.
How do I access the community?
After joining, you'll receive an invite to our private Circle community. This is where members share work, ask questions, and get feedback from peers.
What's the difference between annual and monthly?
The only difference is price. Annual is $290/year (saving you $58, or 2 months free). Monthly is $29/month. Both get the exact same access and benefits.
Do you offer team plans?
Yes. Teams of 3+ get 20% off. Contact us at team@thecreativerecord.com for team pricing.
