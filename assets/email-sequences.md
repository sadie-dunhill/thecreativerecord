## Email Design Guidelines

### Visual Standards
- **Background:** White (#ffffff)
- **Text:** Navy (#1a1a2e) or Dark Gray (#333333)
- **CTA Buttons:** Coral (#e94560), white text
- **Links:** Coral (#e94560), underline on hover
- **Font:** System sans-serif stack
- **Max width:** 600px

### Writing Style
- Direct, conversational
- No exclamation point spam
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Clear single CTA per email
- Signed by Sadie (personal)

### Technical Setup
- Plain text fallback always
- Mobile-responsive
- Dark mode compatible
- Test in Gmail, Apple Mail, Outlook
