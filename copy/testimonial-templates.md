# The Creative Record — Testimonial Templates

---

## How to Gather Real Testimonials

### Timing Matters
Ask for testimonials when the value is freshest:
- **For skills:** 48-72 hours after they've downloaded and applied the first framework
- **For feedback service:** Immediately after delivering the audit/creative review
- **For subscription:** 30 days after joining (they've had time to see ongoing value)

### The Request Script

**Email Template:**

Subject: Quick favor?

Hey [Name],

Glad you're getting value from [Skill/Membership/Service].

Quick favor: Would you be willing to share a brief testimonial about your experience?

Specifically, I'd love to know:
- What problem were you trying to solve?
- What specific result or change have you seen?
- What would you tell someone considering this?

Even 2-3 sentences is perfect. And feel free to be honest—if there's constructive feedback, I want that too.

Thanks for being an early member.

Matthew

---

### What to Ask For

**Good testimonials include:**
1. **Specific before state** (what wasn't working)
2. **Specific result** (what changed—use numbers when possible)
3. **Specific recommendation** (who should use this)

**Bad testimonials:**
- "Great product, highly recommend!" (too vague)
- "Matthew is awesome!" (about the person, not the result)
- "Love the design!" (surface-level, not outcome-based)

### Where to Use Testimonials

- Skill sales pages (1-2 per skill)
- Homepage social proof section
- Email campaigns
- Social media posts
- Checkout page (reduce cart abandonment)

## TESTIMONIAL DO'S AND DON'TS

### DO:
- Ask for specific numbers and metrics
- Request testimonials right after a win
- Edit for clarity (with permission)
- Use full names and company names when possible
- Include role/title for credibility
- Match testimonials to specific pain points on sales pages

### DON'T:
- Make up testimonials (obviously)
- Use testimonials that only praise without specifics
- Feature testimonials from people who don't match your target audience
- Use testimonials that are more than 6 months old without checking if they're still valid
- Put the same testimonial on every page (tailor to context)

## TESTIMONIAL COLLECTION WORKFLOW

### Week 1 Post-Purchase:
Send "How's it going?" email. No ask yet—just check in.

### Week 2 Post-Purchase:
Send the testimonial request using the template above.

### If No Response (Week 3):
Send follow-up: "No worries if you're swamped. If you do have 2 minutes to share your experience later, I'd really appreciate it."

### If They Respond:
1. Thank them immediately
2. Ask permission to use their full name and company
3. Ask if you can edit for length/clarity
4. Save to testimonial library
5. Tag by skill/service used
6. Use on relevant sales pages
