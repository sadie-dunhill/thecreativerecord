Step 1 — Download

After you buy, check your email

Your skills are delivered by email. Here's exactly what to do:

1

Check your inbox

After buying, you'll get a confirmation email within a minute. Subject line: "Your Creative Record Skills Are Ready."

Can't find it? Check your spam/junk folder. If it's still not there after 5 minutes, email sadie@goodostudios.com
2

Click the download link

The email has a button that says "Download Your Skills." Click it. Your browser will download a folder (or a .zip file).

On a phone? The files will save to your Downloads folder. You can also email yourself the link and open it on a computer later.
3

Open the folder

If you downloaded a .zip file, double-click it to open it. This extracts the contents into a regular folder. On a phone, tap it once and it will expand automatically.

Not sure what a .zip file is? It's just a compressed folder. Double-clicking it "unzips" it — like opening a package. Nothing installs, nothing changes on your computer.
4

Open README-FIRST.html

Find the file called "README-FIRST.html" and double-click it. It opens in your web browser (like Safari, Chrome, or Edge). This is your guide for everything else.

What's an .html file? It's a webpage saved on your computer. It opens like a website but you don't need internet to view it. Just double-click.
The only rule: always start with README-FIRST.html

Every skill comes with this guide. It tells you exactly what order to use everything in. If you ever feel lost, close everything, open README-FIRST.html, and start from there.

Step 2 — Open the Documents

Opening .md files (it's easy)

Some files end in ".md" — that just means they're simple text documents. Here's how to open them:

💻

On a Mac

Double-click the .md file. It might open in TextEdit. That's fine — everything is readable. Or right-click it and choose "Open With" → any text editor or even Google Docs.

🖥️

On Windows

Double-click the .md file. It will open in Notepad. Everything is readable there. Or open Word, Google Docs, or Notion and drag the file in.

📱

On iPhone / iPad

Open the Files app, find the downloaded folder, and tap the .md file. It opens as a text file and is completely readable.

🤖

On Android

Tap the .md file from your Downloads folder. Choose "Open as text file" or use the Google Docs app to open it.
